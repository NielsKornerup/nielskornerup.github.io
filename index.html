About Me

I am a third year computer science Ph.D. student at The University of Texas at Austin. I am generally interested in CS theory and my recent work has been focused on time-space lower bounds for classical and quantum computation. I am also broadly interested in quantum, reversible, and other kinds of unconventional computation.

My primary non-academic interests include eurogames and hiking.

You can reach me at nielskorneruputexas.edu.
